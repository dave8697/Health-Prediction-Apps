Check Your BMI Here

This web application will provide you with the facility of detecting diseases with click of a button. The modern approach to healthcare is to prevent the disease with early intervention rather than go for a treatment after diagnosis. As healthcare generates large data, the challenge is to collect this data and effectively use it for analysis, prediction, and treatment. With recent development in technologies such as big data and machine learning, it's possible to get more accurate results for disease prediction. Physicians are teaming up with statisticians and computer scientists to develop better tools to predict the diseases.



Included here is the implementation of web application through feature driven development. Each activity of feature driven development is discussed with artifacts produced during that activity. The web application has been developed using Django framework of python. In this application we have used machine learning (ML) algorithms to predict risk factors for a person’s heart disease, depending on several characteristics of the medical history. Use input features such as gender, cholesterol, blood pressure, TTH, and stress to predict the patient’s risk of heart disease.
